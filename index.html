Privacy Policy for TideWatcher

Last updated: July 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TideWatcher. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

We request “When In Use” location access solely to center the map on your current position and show nearby tide stations. Location data is used in-app and not stored on our servers.

Tide & Station Data

We fetch station lists and tide predictions from the NOAA Tides & Currents API. This data is cached temporarily in-app for performance.

Favorites

We store your favorite station IDs locally in your device’s UserDefaults so they persist across launches. We do not share or sell this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your current location on the map.
  • To fetch and render tide predictions and charts.
  • To save and load your favorites locally.
  • Optionally, to collect anonymous analytics and crash reports if you opt in.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell or rent your personal information. All API calls use HTTPS. Favorites and cached data remain on your device. We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Choices

You can revoke location access in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → TideWatcher. Clearing app data will remove your favorites. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data.

6. Children’s Privacy

TideWatcher is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect changes. Continued app use constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us

For questions, email: support@tidewatchapp.com.
